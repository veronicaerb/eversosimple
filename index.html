Greetings! I'm Veronica Erb.

I research, design, and develop websites.

Through usability testing and non-leading interviews, I learn ways to make the Rwanda Education Commons easier and more helpful for our users in teacher training schools. I take my experience from the field to the office to promote web products that serve the user.

I strive for excellence in my code. Markup is clean, semantic, and compliant from the start, and styles are tidily tucked into stylesheets. When my supervisor once declared, "Your code always makes me happy!" I knew I was on the right track.

My experience in research and code provides a valuable perspective for user interface design. I can imagine how users would react to designs, and I create interactive prototypes with code that can be used in production.

The web is a big, exciting place. I adore being able take a piece and plan it, build it, and find out how to make it better. I constantly seek to build my skills and exchange ideas with the other people of the web. If you are one of them—
